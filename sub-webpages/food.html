Karjalanpiirakka

A simple yet delicious dish, this small rye crust pie is filled with rice porridge or mashed potato and often topped with egg butter. It's a popular snack or light meal in Finland.

Kalakeitto

This hearty and flavorful soup is made with a variety of fish, potatoes, carrots, onions, and other vegetables. It's perfect for warming up on a cold Finnish day.

Pulla

A sweet and aromatic bread, Pulla is flavored with cardamom and often enjoyed with butter and jam. It's a popular treat for breakfast, lunch, or as an afternoon snack.

Riisipuuro

This creamy and comforting rice pudding is often served with lingonberry jam and cinnamon. It's a traditional Finnish dessert that's perfect for any occasion.

Poronkäristys

A hearty and flavorful stew, Poronkäristys is made with reindeer meat, potatoes, carrots, and onions. It's a popular dish in the northern parts of Finland, where reindeer meat is a staple food.

Mustikkapiirakka

This delicious pie is filled with juicy blueberries and topped with a buttery crust. It's a classic Finnish dessert that's perfect for summer.
