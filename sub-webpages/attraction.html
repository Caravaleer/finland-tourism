Northern Lights

Finland's northern location offers stunning views of the Aurora Borealis, or Northern Lights, a natural light display in the sky, primarily seen in high-latitude regions. This celestial phenomenon is a popular tourist attraction, especially during the winter months.

Porvoo

A charming medieval town with colorful wooden houses, cobblestone streets, and a beautiful riverfront. It's a popular day trip destination from Helsinki.

Rovaniemi

The official hometown of Santa Claus, Rovaniemi offers a magical winter experience with snow-covered landscapes, reindeer sled rides, and a visit to Santa's Village.

Turku

Finland's oldest city, Turku is known for its beautiful cathedral, medieval castle, and vibrant cultural scene. It's a great place to explore Finnish history and traditions.

Lake Saimaa

The largest lake in Finland, Lake Saimaa offers stunning scenery, opportunities for boating, fishing, and swimming, and a chance to spot the rare Saimaa seal.

National Parks of Finland

Finland boasts numerous national parks, offering breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and opportunities for hiking, camping, and nature photography.
